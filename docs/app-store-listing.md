## Common Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need Shopify Plus?**
A: No! NDS:RentalRates works on all Shopify plans.

**Q: Can I rent and sell the same product?**
A: Yes! Enable rentals on rental products, leave it off for regular products.

**Q: How does pricing work?**
A: You set a base price per day. Optionally add tiers (e.g., 7+ days = 20% off per day).

**Q: What if someone books dates I'm unavailable?**
A: The calendar shows all bookings. You can also block off dates when items aren't available.

**Q: Does this work with my theme?**
A: Yes! The date picker integrates with any Shopify theme.

**Q: Is there a limit on bookings?**
A: No limits! Handle as many rentals as you need.

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: Only what's needed for rentals: product info, rental dates, and order details. See our privacy policy for full details.

**Q: Can customers cancel or change dates?**
A: They can contact you to modify orders. The app tracks the original reservation.
